CHONGQING FILTER IS?

Chongqing filter is the second open LR/PS preset of Riino Lin. The main spirit comes from a experience editing photo took in Chongqing. This filter is designed to be used in low key.

DERIVATION

The origin preset is for low key photograph. Main style is to simulate Film feelings.
