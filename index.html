ABOUT

Low ill. is?

Low illu. was offically founded on Oct. 1st, 2018, which was a image storage library at that time. Later, it becomes a seperate image provider. The reason of building such isolated image library from my personal entity is that it create a better structure for development and reshape. Low illuminance is providing footages for personal website (RiinoSite),research projects,and other medias. The inner meanning of this is to suggest the darkroom work of photograph.

低照度正式在2018年10月1日成为一个独立的图库。在之后的几年中，基本上作为个人或小型使用的平面素材提供者。 之所以要将其独立出来，而非作为个人直接的版权，是为了结构化管理和优化。low illuminance目前为个人的网站(RiinoSite),科研项目，以及其他微信公众号媒体供图。 低照度的含义可以追溯到传统的暗房摄影工艺，以体现摄影与后期为核心。
